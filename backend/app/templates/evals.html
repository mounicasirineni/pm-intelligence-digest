Guardrails

Pipeline health indicators — not included in the quality score
{% if eval_rows %}
Silent % = sources with no new articles in the lookback window / total configured sources
Fetched = total articles collected across all active sources; exceeds source count when a source publishes multiple articles per day
Confident % = articles summarized with high-medium confidence / total fetched articles; low confidence means the model could not reliably extract signal
Relevant % = articles with high-medium PM relevance / confident articles; low relevance means the content is not useful for PM interview prep
Utilized % = articles cited in the final synthesis / PM relevant articles; measures how much of the eligible pool the synthesizer used
Weak % = paragraphs scoring ≤ 2 on any quality dimension / total paragraphs scored; high values indicate systemic synthesis quality issues
